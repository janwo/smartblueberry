Presence Settings

Adjust the way how Smart Blueberry reacts to presence changes.

Presence

The presence management will automatically adjust the presence state of n area and the entire home in dependence to the last presence detection events. In Smart Blueberry presence_mode reflects the presence status of your entire home and has three states:

  • At home (present)
  • Away (away)
  • Abandoned (abandoned)

In order to trigger presence, your presence entities should be names like <any name> and have the device_class attribute set to motion.

You can adjust the number of hours until presence management sets the presence state to away or abandoned by changing the values below.
